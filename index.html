Introduction

Hobbies

Music

"In a world filled with hate, we must still dare to hope. In a world filled with anger, we must still dare to comfort. In a world filled with despair, we must still dare to dream. And in a world filled with distrust, we must still dare to believe."

Those were the thoughtful words said by my idol and favorite artist, Michael Jackson. He was not only a musical genius and the most famous entertainer of all time, Michael Jackson was also one of the most successul individuals in the world and an inspiration on so many levels. The small poor kid from Gary Indiana not only affected the music history by his innovative music videos, choregraphies and fashion, but he also had a global impact through some of his compositions like "Heal The World", "We Are The World", "Man In The Mirror", ""Earth Song", "They Don't Care About Us", "Black Or White" etc.His work didn't cease at revolutionizing music, he was also a great humanitarian and cared for charity through philanthropic activities such as his "Heal The World Foundation", making hospitals visits, donatings million of dollars etc.

I am also a huge fan of rock music and artists like Marilyn Manson, Rammstein, Depeche Mode just to name a few, and I am lucky enough to have had the chance to see a few of them live !
